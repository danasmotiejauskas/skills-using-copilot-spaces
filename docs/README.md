## Overview: Project Management Processes

OctoAcme follows a structured, lifecycle-based project management approach that emphasizes customer value, iterative delivery, and clear accountability. Projects move through five phases: Initiation, Planning, Execution, Release, and Close & Retrospective.

**Core roles include:**
- Project Manager (PM): Coordinates delivery, schedules, risk, and communication.
- Product Manager (PdM): Defines outcomes, prioritizes backlog, measures success.
- Developers: Build and test features to quality standards.
- QA/Testing: Validates quality and acceptance.
- Stakeholders: Provide input, approvals, and strategic direction.

Key workflows include prioritized backlogs, sprint/iteration planning, daily standups, weekly delivery syncs, and structured release processes. Quality assurance is embedded via automated tests, security scanning, and manual QA as needed. Risks are tracked in a register, escalated through defined paths, and reviewed regularly. Continuous improvement is enabled through retrospectives, actionable learnings, and regular celebration of wins and progress.

**Principles:**
- Customer-first
- Iterative delivery
- Clear ownership
- Data-informed decisions
- Psychological safety
